Beef & Potato Stew Recipe

Description

This is the recipe for Beef & Potato Stew

1. Pan sear two packets of beef

2. Season the Beef with Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Onion Powder

3. Cut the potatoes

4. Once beef is cooked thrown in the potatoes

5. Add Beef Broth

6. Add Cheese

7. Simmer till finished

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • Potatoes
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Onion Powder, Cheese, Beef Broth
